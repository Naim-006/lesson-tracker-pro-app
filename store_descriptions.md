# Lesson Tracker Pro - Store Descriptions

## Google Play Store Description (Short)
CRM, diary and bookkeeping for driving instructors. Manage pupils, schedule lessons, track payments, and monitor progress all in one app.

## Google Play Store Description (Full)

**Lesson Tracker Pro** is the ultimate business management tool designed specifically for driving instructors. Streamline your driving school operations with powerful features for pupil management, lesson scheduling, financial tracking, and progress monitoring.

**Key Features:**

👥 **Pupil Management**
- Comprehensive pupil profiles with contact details, pickup/dropoff locations
- Track pupil status (current, waiting, passed, archived)
- Monitor lesson history and progress
- Customizable skill categories for progress tracking

📅 **Smart Diary & Scheduling**
- Visual calendar with intuitive lesson booking
- Schedule individual lessons or create recurring slots
- Offer open slots to pupils in your area
- Sync with external calendars (Google, Apple, Outlook)
- Set working hours and availability

💰 **Financial Management**
- Track income and expenses with detailed categorization
- Record payments with receipt uploads
- Monitor mileage for tax purposes
- Generate financial reports and exports
- Multi-currency support

📊 **Progress Tracking**
- Customizable skill categories and syllabus
- Track pupil progress across multiple skills
- Generate progress reports
- Test result tracking and analysis

🔔 **Activity & Communication**
- Built-in messaging system for pupil communication
- Activity feed for all app events
- Notification system for important updates

⚙️ **Customizable Settings**
- Set your own pricing and lesson lengths
- Configure vehicle details
- Customize business terms and conditions
- COVID-19 safety risk assessment
- Theme customization with dark mode support

**Why Choose Lesson Tracker Pro?**

- **Save Time:** Automate administrative tasks and focus on teaching
- **Stay Organized:** All your pupil data, schedules, and finances in one place
- **Professional:** Impress pupils with organized, professional service
- **Data-Driven:** Track progress and identify areas for improvement
- **Flexible:** Customize the app to match your teaching style

**Perfect For:**
- ADI (Approved Driving Instructors)
- PDI (Potential Driving Instructors)
- Driving school owners
- Independent driving instructors

Download Lesson Tracker Pro today and transform your driving instruction business!
